▶ What is this website about:
This website is about spreading awareness among people to be more open to what's happening to our trees and it also to show how we can help by donating or by planting our own trees.


▶ Why is it important for us to know how to care and what actions we can do to save trees:
Trees produces what we called oxygen that we as humans breathe it every day, that is why it is important for us to know how to take proper care for our trees. (UN FAO) "Quoted over 10 million hectares of forest are cut down each year".
